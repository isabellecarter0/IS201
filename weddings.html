Favorite Wedding Venues

Here are some of my favorite Utah wedding venues!

  1. Amangiri, Utah
    • Accommodation design features include white stone floors, concrete walls, natural timbers and fittings in blackened steel.
    • Integrates and magnifies Utah's desert landscape
    • Located in Southern, Utah near the border of Arizona


  2. The Summit
    • Located on 11,000-foot Hidden Peak with 360-degree views of Utah's stunning Wasatch Mountains
    • Has an indoor, wooden dance floor and floor-to-ceiling windows


  3. Sorrel River Ranch
    • Located in Moab, Utah, complete with a full property byuout
    • Known for its unforgettable, private location, perfect for complete immersion
